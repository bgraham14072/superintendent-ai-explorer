# The AI-Powered Superintendent

An interactive Explorer of 30 AI workflows for public school superintendents,
organized by the eight domains where the superintendent's work actually lives
— board governance, strategic planning, labor negotiations, crisis comms,
curriculum, pupil personnel, HR, and finance — and banded by the size of the
district you run.

**Live site:** https://bgraham14072.github.io/superintendent-ai-explorer/

## Why district size matters

A superintendent of 450 students is the curriculum director, the HR director,
and often the building principal on any given morning. A superintendent of
40,000 is running a small city. The workflows that help are not the same.
The Explorer offers a size filter (< 500, < 1,000, < 3,000, < 8,000, 15,000+)
and a dedicated "By District Size" view that groups each workflow with the
bands it is designed for.

## Featured workflows (the exclusive-to-superintendent duties)

- **Executive Session Memo & Decision Log** — a disciplined brief and record
  that protects the board, the district, and the superintendent.
- **Portrait of a Graduate → Budget Alignment** — walk every budget line
  against the Portrait competencies. Make the Portrait real.
- **CBA Cost-Out & Comparator Scan** — multi-year cost modeling and
  comparator analysis that gives the board confidence and the union a common
  language.
- **Crisis Comms — First 90 Minutes** — the window that defines the
  aftermath. Pre-thought structure survives the adrenaline.

## Tools covered

ChatGPT Advanced Voice Mode, Claude, NotebookLM, ChatGPT.
